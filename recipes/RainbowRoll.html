Description

A colorful sushi roll filled with avocado, cucumber, salmon, tuna, and yellowtail.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1/2 cup sushi vinegar
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 teaspoon wasabi powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 pound salmon, sliced
  • 1/2 pound tuna, sliced
  • 1/2 pound yellowtail, sliced
  • 1 sheet nori

Steps

  1. Cook the sushi rice according to the package directions.
  2. While the rice is cooking, combine the sushi vinegar, mayonnaise, wasabi powder, and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, let it cool slightly. Then, stir in the vinegar mixture.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat. Spread a thin layer of the rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top.
  5. Place a layer of avocado, cucumber, salmon, tuna, and yellowtail on the rice.
  6. Roll up the sushi tightly, using the bamboo mat to help you.
  7. Cut the roll into 6-8 pieces.
  8. Enjoy!
